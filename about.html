01   THE STORY
SY/STORY · NEW ERA
SY · BR / HERITAGE

A KZN
institution.
A new chapter.

Standard Yoke has been part of the KwaZulu-Natal construction industry since 1968. From the early 1980s the Walker family built the business into one of the region's most respected and relationship-driven roof truss manufacturers — known for engineering, MiTek-approved processes and a phone call that meant something. In that time, an estimated 45 000 roofs — over 6.5 million m² — have gone up over KZN homes, factories and farms on Standard Yoke trusses.

In 2026, under new ownership, the business is entering a new era. The mission is unchanged: dependable manufacturing, honest communication, on-time delivery. But the offer has expanded — alongside trusses, Standard Yoke now stocks and supplies a growing range of roofing and building materials, and can connect you with accredited installers to put the roof up. One supplier, one truck, always on time.

The goal is simple. Less admin for builders. Fewer suppliers to manage. Fewer trucks on site. More time on the build.
